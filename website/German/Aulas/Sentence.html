Lessons

Sentence structure
Satzstruktur

Position

Subject Verb Rest of the phrase
Ich bin Bruno
Ich wohne in England

As you can see in these phrases, the verbs are in the second position. In affirmative phrases the verb always come in the second position. But notice that in Heute bin ich in Berlin the subject ich is not at the first position.

W questions/W-Fragen

W-Fragen Verb Rest of the phrase
Wo wohnen Sie?
Wie heißen Sie?
Woher kommen Sie?

The same thing can be seen here, the verb takes the second position even making a question.

Yes/no questions / Ja/Nein Fragen

Verb Subect Rest of the phrase
Heißen Sie Emilia?
Hast du Schokolade?

As you can see, the yes/no questions are a bit different, the verb comes in the first position.
